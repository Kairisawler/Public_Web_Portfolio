What is a Web Portfolio?

Portfolios are a unique document, or in this case website, that demonstrates skills, achievements and past works to potential employers or clients! Most portfolios include things such as: a skill inventory; a resume; working history; awards/certifications; education; and past projects/evidence of said skills!
